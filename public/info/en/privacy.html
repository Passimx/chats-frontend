Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

PassimX is an open-source messaging platform focused on minimal data collection. This document explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data We Store

  • Messages: stored indefinitely unless manually deleted by system administrators.
  • Backend error logs: may include technical error data, but do not contain IP addresses or user identifiers.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No IP addresses
  • No user-agent strings
  • No location or device identifiers
  • No analytics tracking

3. Local Data Stored in Your Browser

The following may be stored locally on your device:

  • LocalStorage data
  • IndexedDB data
  • CacheStorage
  • Service Worker data

This data never leaves your device unless explicitly sent by you.

4. Third-Party Services

PassimX may integrate with third-party wallet providers (e.g., TON Connect). These providers may collect data under their own policies. We do not control their behavior.

5. Children’s Privacy

The service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Data Deletion

Developers may delete stored messages or logs at any time due to technical or operational reasons.

7. No Guarantee

The service is experimental. We cannot guarantee the confidentiality, durability, or availability of stored data.
